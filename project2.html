Personal Identity

A website that highlights photographs.

As a photographer I wanted to design and build a simple yet informative website that showcased my work and highlighted the photographs above all. It needed to be modern, bold and playful so it could stand out among the different types of photography portfolios. Also, I really wanted to take care of every single detail in order to have an unique look.

On the one hand, the website was designed with the idea of popping out the photographs harmoniously. So I wanted to implement light boxes on a single page layout. Giving the website the ability to have a flexible yet simple setting. On the other hand, after some concepting, I was inspired by an illustration of a self-portrait I did previously on Adobe Illustrator, this particular illustaration has a very low poly style and plays a lot with color shades. So I wanted this website to have the same feeling by using a vibrant colour scheme and a bold typography.

  • Type: Web design, web development

  • Client: Personal

  • Date: 2013

Vibrant colors

I wanted to play with an energizing and lively color palette. After looking at many different hues and combinations, I picked a set of three colours that communicated clear meaning, worked harmoniously, and provided the energy to express my own brand.

Sharp and focus

Since it is a website that highlights images, I wanted to keep the text content reduced but at the same time clear and sharp. That is why I wanted to give this website a lot of white space between images and descriptions. By using this typography I provide the importance that headlines need, always keeping a typographic contrast.

Start somewhere

In order to have have a clear vision of what I wanted this website to look like, I started sketching some pages. It was important to define the core features, such as the lightbox slideshow, and the parked features, such as the FAQ and blog, to not being overwhelmed by the project. Then I could begin the expansive ideation stage again, but within a narrower realm.
